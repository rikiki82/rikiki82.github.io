♥ Cookies ♥

For ~16 cookies (35 minutes)
  • 500g of flour
  • 200g of blond sugar
  • 200g of brown sugar
  • salt
  • 1 yeast packet
  • 2 eggs
  • 250g of butter
  • vanilla extract
  • chocolate chip/nutella/peanut butter
  1. In a big bowl add the flour, sugars, a pinch of salt, the yeast and stir well.
  2. Melt the butter in an other bowl. Then add the eggs and the vanilla extract, scramble the mixture.
  3. Pour the mixture into the big bowl and mix well.
  4. If you have the time it's better to let the dough rest one hour in the fridge.
  5. Shape your cookies on parchment paper and add whatever you want inside them.
  6. Cook for 10 minutes in the oven at 200°C.

Crepes

For 20 crepes (15 minutes)
  • 500g of flour
  • 5 eggs
  • 1l of milk
  • 3 tablespoons of oil
  • 1/2 glass of beer
  • 2 tablespoons of rhum
  • vanilla extract
  1. In a bowl, pour half of the flour, then add the eggs one by one, then add a little milk, then flour, then milk again and so on.
  2. Add the oil, beer, vanilla and rhum.
  3. Leave to rest for 1 hour, your dough is ready.

Brownie

For 3 persons (50 minutes)
  • 100g of dark chocolate
  • 3 eggs
  • 40g of butter
  • 40g of sugar
  1. Melt the chocolate and butter in the microwave and mix well. Let cool a little and during this time, mix the sugar and the eggs with a whisk.
  2. Incorporate the chocolate-butter mixture into the sugar-egg mixture and stir well until your dough is smooth.
  3. Butter and flour a mold about 20 cm in diameter and pour in the preparation.
  4. Bake the preparation in the oven for about 30 minutes at 180 ° C.
  5. Let cool and cut the cake into small rectangles to give them the shape of brownies.

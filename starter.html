Kiwi and tomato salad

For 1 person (10 minutes)
  • 1 kiwi
  • 1 tomato
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • lemon
  • oil
  1. Peel the kiwis and tomatoes, cut them into slices.
  2. Arrange in a dish, season with salt, drizzle with a little oil, add the juice of half a lemon.
  3. For a salad of kiwis alone, you can do without vinegar or lemon, they are sometimes quite acidic.

Pumpkin and carrot soup

For 4 persons (~1 hour)
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • parsley
  • 1 kg of pumpkin
  • 500g of carrots
  • 2 potatoes
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 500ml of milk
  • 500ml of chicken stock
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • nutmeg
  1. Peel and dice the pumpkin, potatoes and carrots into slices.
  2. Mince the garlic and onion.
  3. Sweat the onion in olive oil.
  4. Add all the vegetables and garlic then pour in the broth and milk.
  5. Season with salt, pepper and nutmeg and cook for about thirty minutes.
  6. Mix everything and adjust the seasoning if necessary.
